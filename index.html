About

Bone Brothel is a rock/grunge/metal band based in Portland, OR. Blending haunting melodies, guttural riffs, and ferocious vocals, the band draws influence from icons like Soundgarden, Black Sabbath, Nirvana, and The Pretty Reckless. Their music fuses heavy energy with raw emotion, creating a sonic experience that’s both visceral and hypnotic. Members include Alexis on bass, Shannon on drums, Gea on vocals, and Calvin on lead guitar.
